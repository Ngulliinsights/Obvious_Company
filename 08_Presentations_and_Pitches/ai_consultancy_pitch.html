Risk Management & Mitigation Strategies

Risk: Execution Consistency Challenges

Historical pattern of strong analytical thinking but inconsistent implementation

Mitigation: Systematic Minimum Requirements

Weekly minimum activities designed to be manageable regardless of emotional state, with built-in accountability systems and progress tracking

Reduced demand for professional development services during economic uncertainty

Mitigation: Revenue Diversification

Multiple revenue streams (individual, corporate, ongoing support) with different economic sensitivities, plus pivot capability to AI implementation consulting

Risk: Technology Evolution Outpacing Curriculum

Rapid AI advancement making course content obsolete

Mitigation: Modular Curriculum Design

Quarterly content updates, focus on principles rather than specific tools, and ongoing support services keeping clients current with developments
