Systematic approach to designing learning experiences that respect professional maturity while building AI-enhanced capabilities

The Curriculum Development Framework revolutionizes professional education by moving beyond traditional training models to create sophisticated learning experiences that augment existing expertise with AI capabilities. This approach recognizes that accomplished professionals require fundamentally different educational strategies than entry-level learners.
